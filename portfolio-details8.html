Project information

  • Category: JAVA GUI
  • Project Title: Hairdresser Booking app
  • Project date: 10 OCT, 2020

Task:
This system is to develop an automated booking platform for customers and hairdresser.
Customers are provided with the capability of booking at hair salon or hairdresser of they want without the hassle of long waiting.
At the same time, for hairdresser real-time booking management is enabled through this application that improves the business processes of hair salons by providing them an online platform for the salon to reach potential customers.

The hairdresser inputs the username, email and password which is validated and authenticated on the server side.
